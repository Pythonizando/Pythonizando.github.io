Privacy Policy

App: Pico y Placa Pasto  ·  Developer: Sorencorp  ·  Last updated: March 12, 2026

Sorencorp ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Pico y Placa Pasto Android application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly. The App stores the following data locally on your device only using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device:

  • Vehicle license plate digits and custom names you enter
  • Color preferences assigned to each vehicle
  • Notification preferences (enabled / disabled)

2. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET – required to display ads via Google AdMob.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – used to send optional daily reminders at 7:00 a.m. when one of your registered vehicles has a restriction that day. This permission is optional and can be revoked at any time from your device settings.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – used to reschedule notifications after the device restarts.
  • VIBRATE – used for vibration when notifications are delivered.

3. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • IP address and general location
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data

For more information on how Google uses this data, please review Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party SDK:

  • Google AdMob – advertising SDK by Google LLC. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

All data (vehicle entries, notification settings) is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data from your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App uses interest-based advertising and is not suitable for audiences under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
