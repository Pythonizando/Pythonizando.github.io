Privacy Policy

App: Sudoku – Logic Puzzle Game  ·  Developer: Sorencorp  ·  Last updated: March 11, 2026

Sorencorp ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Sudoku – Logic Puzzle Game Android application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly. The App stores game progress and personal records (best times and player name) locally on your device only using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

2. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • IP address and general location
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data

For more information on how Google uses this data, please review Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party SDK:

  • Google AdMob – advertising SDK by Google LLC. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

All game data (records, progress) is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data from your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App uses interest-based advertising and is not suitable for audiences under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
