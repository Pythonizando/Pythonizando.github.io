## 1. Introduction

SorenGBA ("the App") is a Game Boy Advance emulator for Android developed by SorenCorp. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when you use the App and how it is used.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

SorenGBA does **not** directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

However, the App uses **Google AdMob** to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect the following data automatically:

- Device identifiers (advertising ID)
- IP address (approximate location)
- App usage data and interaction with ads
- Device information (model, OS version)

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC under their own privacy policy.

---

## 3. ROM Files

The App allows users to import local ROM files (.gba) from their device. These files are:

- Stored **locally on your device only**
- Never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server
- Never accessed by SorenCorp

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party service:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Google AdMob | Banner advertising | https://policies.google.com/privacy |

We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy to understand how AdMob handles your data.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

SorenGBA does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The App is rated Everyone (E) and does not target children specifically.

---

## 6. Data Retention

SorenGBA stores only your app preferences (control layout, settings) locally on your device via Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data collected by AdMob (access, deletion, opt-out of personalized ads). You can manage ad personalization at:
https://adssettings.google.com
